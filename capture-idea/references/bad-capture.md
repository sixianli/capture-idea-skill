## Why This Fails

- "Core Ideas" is just a topic list, not an idea capture.
- "Thought Trajectory" is flat chronology with no pivot or insight.
- "Verbatim Quotes" are too short and carry no strategic meaning.
- The result would not help someone remember why the discussion mattered.
